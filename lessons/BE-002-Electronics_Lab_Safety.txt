============================================================

English
A beginner may think small batteries and LEDs are always harmless. But short circuits, reverse polarity, overheated batteries, sharp tools and careless wiring can damage parts or cause injury.

हिन्दी

============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
Lab safety is the first skill of electronics. Most beginner circuits use low voltage, but unsafe habits can still damage components or tools. A safe learner always checks the workspace, disconnects power before rewiring, keeps liquids away, uses correct polarity and stops immediately if there is heat, smoke or burning smell.

Important safety habits:
1. Keep the workspace clean and dry.
2. Keep food and drinks away.
3. Turn power OFF before changing wires.
4. Never short battery terminals.
5. Check + and − polarity before powering.
6. Do not touch hot soldering irons or heated parts.
7. Wear eye protection when cutting component leads.
8. Ask Spark before continuing if something behaves unexpectedly.

Safety is not fear. Safety is control.

हिन्दी
लैब सुरक्षा इलेक्ट्रॉनिक्स का पहला कौशल है। शुरुआती सर्किट सामान्यतः कम वोल्टेज के होते हैं, लेकिन गलत आदतें घटकों और उपकरणों को नुकसान पहुँचा सकती हैं। सुरक्षित विद्यार्थी हमेशा कार्यक्षेत्र जाँचता है, वायर बदलने से पहले पावर OFF करता है, तरल पदार्थ दूर रखता है, सही ध्रुवीयता उपयोग करता है और गर्मी, धुआँ या जलने की गंध होने पर तुरंत रुकता है।

महत्वपूर्ण आदतें:
1. कार्यक्षेत्र साफ और सूखा रखें।
2. खाना-पीना दूर रखें।
3. वायर बदलने से पहले पावर OFF करें।
4. बैटरी टर्मिनल शॉर्ट न करें।
5. + और − ध्रुवीयता जाँचें।
6. गर्म सोल्डरिंग आयरन या गर्म पार्ट न छुएँ।
7. लीड काटते समय आँखों की सुरक्षा करें।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Inspect your desk. Remove water bottles, food, unnecessary metal objects and clutter. Place your tools on one side and components in a container. Take a photo and ask Spark: 'Is my workspace safe?'

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Changing jumper wires while power is ON.
• Keeping tea, coffee or water near circuits.
• Shorting a battery with wire.
• Using damaged wires or leaking batteries.
• Ignoring heat or burning smell.

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• If a component gets hot, turn power OFF immediately.
• If a battery heats up, disconnect it and do not reuse it until checked.
• If you smell burning, stop and ask Spark to review your wiring.
• If a tool is sharp or hot, place it in a safe zone.
