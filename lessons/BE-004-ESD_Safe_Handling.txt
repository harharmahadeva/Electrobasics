============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
ESD means Electrostatic Discharge. Sensitive parts such as ICs, microcontrollers, MOSFETs and sensors should be stored in anti-static bags and handled by edges. ESD mats and wrist straps safely discharge static.

हिन्दी
ESD का अर्थ Electrostatic Discharge है। संवेदनशील घटकों को एंटी-स्टैटिक बैग में रखें और किनारों से पकड़ें।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Practice holding PCB by edges and storing ICs safely.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Touching IC pins
• Normal plastic bags for ICs
• Working on carpet
• No wrist strap

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• Touch grounded metal before handling
• Use ESD mat if possible
• Store ICs in anti-static bags
