============================================================ REAL LIFE
PROBLEM ============================================================

Imagine turning on a garden hose.

Higher water pressure pushes more water. A narrower pipe reduces the
flow.

Electrical circuits behave the same way.

Voltage pushes. Resistance restricts. Current is the flow.

============================================================ THEORY
============================================================

Ohm’s Law describes the relationship between:

V = Voltage (Volt)

I = Current (Ampere)

R = Resistance (Ohm)

Formula

V = I × R

From this:

I = V ÷ R

R = V ÷ I

Simple Example

Battery = 9V

Resistor = 1kΩ

Current is small.

Now replace the resistor with 220Ω.

Current increases.

============================================================ OHM’S LAW
TRIANGLE ============================================================

        V
      -----
      I | R

Cover the quantity you want to calculate.

============================================================ QUIZ
============================================================

1.  Ohm’s Law formula? Answer: V = I × R.

2.  Unit of resistance? Answer: Ohm (Ω).

3.  Increasing resistance usually? Answer: Decreases current.

4.  Increasing voltage usually? Answer: Increases current.

5.  Which three quantities are related? Answer: Voltage, Current and
    Resistance.
