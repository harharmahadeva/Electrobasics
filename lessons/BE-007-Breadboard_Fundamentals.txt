============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
A breadboard is a reusable prototyping board. Five holes in each terminal row are connected internally. The center gap separates sides. Power rails carry + and −, but some rails are split and must be checked.

हिन्दी
ब्रेडबोर्ड पुन: उपयोग योग्य बोर्ड है। पाँच छेद अंदर से जुड़े होते हैं।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Insert wires and find connected rows.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Thinking all holes connect
• Ignoring center gap
• Power rails not continuous
• Loose parts

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• Use continuity mode to test holes
• Check rails before power
• Keep wires short
