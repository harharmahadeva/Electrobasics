LESSON PURPOSE
============================================================

This lesson is written for a learner who may have never touched electronic components before. Keep all explanations practical, visual and friendly. The lesson must combine text, real images, diagrams, Spark AI support and hands-on activity.

यह पाठ ऐसे विद्यार्थी के लिए है जिसने शायद पहले कभी इलेक्ट्रॉनिक घटकों को हाथ नहीं लगाया। भाषा सरल, दृश्य और प्रैक्टिकल होनी चाहिए।

============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
Hand tools include flush cutter, long-nose plier, wire stripper, tweezers, screwdriver set, helping hands, magnifier and safety glasses. Use the correct tool for cutting, bending, stripping, holding and tightening.

हिन्दी
हैंड टूल्स में कटर, प्लायर, स्ट्रिपर, चिमटी, स्क्रूड्राइवर आदि शामिल हैं।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

============================================================
PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Bend resistor leads, trim LED legs and strip wire.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Cutting toward face
• Using scissors
• Pulling components by force
• Wrong screwdriver

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• Part too small: use tweezers
• Wire damaged: use proper stripper slot
• Lead won't fit: bend gently
