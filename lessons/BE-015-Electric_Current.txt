REAL-LIFE PROBLEM | वास्तविक जीवन की समस्या
============================================================

English
Devices work only when charge actually flows.

हिन्दी
उपकरण तभी काम करते हैं जब धारा बहती है।

Teaching Note:
Start the lesson with this problem. Do not begin with a definition. Beginners learn faster when they first understand why the topic matters.

============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
Current is the flow of electric charge. Symbol I, unit Ampere. Small current is measured in mA. Current flows in closed circuits and must be measured in series.

हिन्दी
धारा विद्युत आवेश का प्रवाह है। इकाई एम्पियर है।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Measure LED current by inserting DMM in series.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Measuring in parallel
• Wrong current socket
• Forgetting circuit break
• Leaving probe in A port

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• Zero: open circuit or meter not series
• Fuse blown: unsafe current measurement
