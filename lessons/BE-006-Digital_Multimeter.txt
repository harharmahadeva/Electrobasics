============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
A Digital Multimeter measures voltage, current, resistance, continuity and diode behavior. Important parts: display, rotary selector, COM port, VΩ port, mA/10A ports, probes. Black goes to COM; red goes to the correct measuring port.

हिन्दी
डिजिटल मल्टीमीटर वोल्टेज, करंट, रेसिस्टेंस, कंटिन्युटी और डायोड टेस्ट करता है।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Measure AA, AAA and 9V batteries in DC voltage mode.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Wrong port
• Wrong mode
• Current measured like voltage
• Red probe left in 10A

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• Negative reading: reverse probes
• Zero reading: check mode/battery
• OL: wrong range/open path
