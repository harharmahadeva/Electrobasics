BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
Electronics is the practice of controlling electricity using components such as resistors, LEDs, capacitors, sensors, ICs and microcontrollers. Electronics is present in mobile phones, laptops, routers, washing machines, cars, medical devices, robots and satellites.

ElectroBasics is designed as a practical learning journey. You will not only read theory; you will see real components, study clear infographics, use interactive simulations, build circuits on a breadboard, answer quizzes and ask Spark whenever you get stuck.

The course begins with safety and tools because beginners should know how to work correctly before connecting power. After that, you will learn electricity, voltage, current, resistance, Ohm's Law, real electronic components, circuit building, measurements, troubleshooting and mini projects.

The most important rule: you do not need to memorize everything on day one. Electronics becomes easy when you see, touch, build, test and repeat.

हिन्दी
इलेक्ट्रॉनिक्स वह विधा है जिसमें रेसिस्टर, LED, कैपेसिटर, सेंसर, IC और माइक्रोकंट्रोलर जैसे घटकों की सहायता से बिजली को नियंत्रित किया जाता है। यह मोबाइल, लैपटॉप, राउटर, वॉशिंग मशीन, कार, मेडिकल उपकरण, रोबोट और उपग्रहों में मौजूद है।

ElectroBasics एक प्रैक्टिकल सीखने की यात्रा है। यहाँ आप केवल सिद्धांत नहीं पढ़ेंगे; आप वास्तविक घटक देखेंगे, साफ इन्फोग्राफिक समझेंगे, इंटरैक्टिव सिमुलेशन उपयोग करेंगे, ब्रेडबोर्ड पर सर्किट बनाएँगे, क्विज़ हल करेंगे और अटकने पर Spark से पूछेंगे।

कोर्स सुरक्षा और उपकरणों से शुरू होता है क्योंकि पावर जोड़ने से पहले सही तरीके से काम करना सीखना ज़रूरी है। फिर आप बिजली, वोल्टेज, धारा, प्रतिरोध, ओम का नियम, वास्तविक घटक, सर्किट बनाना, मापन, समस्या समाधान और मिनी प्रोजेक्ट सीखेंगे।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Find five electronic devices at home. For each device, write what it does, how it gets power, and what kind of electronics may be inside. Example: TV remote uses batteries and probably has a small PCB, buttons, LED and IC.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• Thinking electronics is only for engineers.
• Trying to jump directly into advanced robotics or AI before learning basics.
• Skipping safety and tools.
• Memorizing words without building or observing real circuits.

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• If the course feels too large, Spark should split it into weekly goals.
• If the learner has no tools yet, Spark should suggest the minimum starter kit.
• If the learner is afraid of making mistakes, Spark should remind them that beginner circuits are designed to be safe and guided.
