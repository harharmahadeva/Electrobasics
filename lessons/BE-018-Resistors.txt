============================================================
BEGINNER-FRIENDLY THEORY | शुरुआती सिद्धांत
============================================================

English
A resistor is a passive component that limits current. Types include carbon composition, carbon film, metal film, metal oxide, wirewound, fusible, SMD, LDR, thermistor, varistor and resistor arrays. Resistors also have tolerance and power rating.

हिन्दी
रेसिस्टर धारा सीमित करने वाला निष्क्रिय घटक है।

Teacher Tone:
Use short sentences. Avoid unnecessary formulas unless the lesson needs them. Whenever a new technical word appears, Spark should explain it using the glossary.

PRACTICAL LAB | प्रैक्टिकल लैब
============================================================

Measure five resistors and test brightness with LED.

Safety Reminder:
Before any wiring or measurement, power must be OFF unless the step explicitly says to power ON. Check polarity. Ask Spark if unsure.

============================================================
COMMON BEGINNER MISTAKES | सामान्य शुरुआती गलतियाँ
============================================================

• No resistor with LED
• Wattage confused with value
• Ignoring tolerance
• Powered measurement

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE | समस्या समाधान
============================================================

• LED bright/hot: increase resistance
• Resistor hot: check power rating
• Value unknown: use DMM
